About Hack Club

Hack Club is a place for students to learn coding in a fun and friendly way. Instead of only listening to lessons, you get to build real projects like websites, apps, and even hardware. The goal is to learn by making things.

You don’t need to be an expert to join. Everyone is welcome — whether you are just starting or already know some coding. At Hack Club, you can share your ideas, ask questions, and create amazing projects with others.
